Moving Average Convergence Divergence

BY AUTHOR

Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) - a strong trend-following momentum analysing tool. It calculates the movement of two Moving Averages and provides signals by an additional Moving Average, called the signal line. The MACD can also be used in many different ways.

Cumulative Delta Volume

BY AUTHOR

Cumulative Delta Volume (CDV) - a very powerful analysing tool when it comes to the Market Volume Flow. It calculates the difference (Delta) of the market bulls and market bears and provides a cumulative overview. It is very strong when you catch Divergences or Absorbtions.
